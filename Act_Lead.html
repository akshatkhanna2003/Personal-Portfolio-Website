Western University - Engineering Design Showcase

• Designed and invented an outdoor game for Kimberly Outdoor Learning Society (KOLS) which can be played in all weather conditions and educate the children

• The game was a scavenger hunt in which kids searched for specific items found in nature, the items differed for the different seasons of the year

• Presented our game to judges present at the showcase including teaching assistants, professors, and engineers

• The game was sent to KOLS to give them an opportunity to play the game
